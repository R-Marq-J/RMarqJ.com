About

The Builder Behind the Systems

I like taking messy, frustrating problems and turning them into clear workflows, useful tools, and systems that actually work.

Background

I'm Marq — a CompTIA A+ certified IT professional and Computer Science student at St. Louis Community College. My path into tech didn't start in a classroom or a corporate environment. It started with curiosity, trial and error, broken setups, home lab experiments, and a desire to understand how things really work underneath the surface.

I've also spent years working inside a small family-run operation, where I learned how messy real systems can get when there's no structure. That experience shaped how I think: good systems should make life easier, reduce confusion, and help people do better work.

What I Do

I run a Proxmox-based home lab where I test infrastructure, self-host services, configure Linux systems, and document what I learn. This website is part of that process too — deployed through a pipeline I built myself instead of just using a drag-and-drop template.

Right now I'm focused on IT support, systems administration, AI tooling, and automation — and especially the bridge between them. I'm drawn to tools that make work more reliable, repeatable, and efficient.

How I Think

I believe in proof over promises. I don't just want to say I'm technical — I want to show the projects, labs, documentation, and deployments behind the claim. I'd rather build a working system than talk about one, and I'd rather automate a repeated task than keep fighting the same problem every week.

My goal is simple: keep learning, keep building, and turn technical skill into systems that create real value.
